Our Road Bikes? Expect the Pinnacle of Comfort and Performance.

For the rider seeking the pinnacle of performance, feel, and pure enjoyment, a Sarto represents the last word in handbuilt carbon fibre racing bikes.

Sarto frames are uniquely customizable. From the model you choose to the size of each tube and how it colours the road, our frames blend unmatched personalisation with obsessive attention to detail.

We craft our frames exclusively from premium carbon fibre and handbuild everything in-house at our HQ in Venice, Italy. Lightweight and immeasurably versatile, we form and tune each tube for a specific task, variously rigid, responsive or any other combination of attributes in service of the needs of each rider.

The Measure of Perfection
Each bike we bring to life is a work of extreme precision, care and creativity. Our close-knit team of artisans take pride in working at the forefront of composites R&D and practical application, backed by decades of experience hand-building bespoke bike frames that ride like a dream.

If you're ready to start your Sarto story, call, email or schedule an appointment at our Venice HQ, and let us bring your unique bike to life.

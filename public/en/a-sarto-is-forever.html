A Sarto is forever

With Sarto, lifetime assistance is guaranteed. Because we have confidence in our products. Because repairing means giving value and having respect for what has been done.

Because extending the life cycle of our bicycles is a way to protect the environment. An idea of environmental sustainability that does not consist in is slogans, certifications or proclamations, but just in doing it.

“We are craftsmen, we take care of the environment our way: using our hands”.


And there is more. The repair of carbon frames is a service we offer to all cyclists, not just our customers.
